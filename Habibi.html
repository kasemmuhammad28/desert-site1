Sahara

The Sahara Desert is the world's largest hot desert and the third largest desert behind Antarctica and the Arctic. Located in North Africa. It covers 9,200,000 square kilometers which is comparable to the are of China or the US!

Western Desert

The Western Desert covers about 700,000 square kilometres and accounts for about two-thirds of Egypt's land area.

Al Bagawat

There is a Coptic Christian cemetery containing hundreds of tombs dating from the 4th to 6th century AD

Pyramid of Giza

Also called Great Pyramid of Khufu, located on a rocky plateau on the west bank of the Nile River in northern Egypt.

Kalahari Desert

A featureless, gently undulating, sand-covered plain, which everywhere is 3,000 feet (900 metres) or more above sea level.
